Introducing myself

Greetings! I am Dr. Pooja Bharti, a seasoned pediatrician committed to fostering the well-being of our youngest generation. With eight years of hands-on experience, I bring a wealth of knowledge and passion to the field of pediatrics. My primary focus areas include child nutrition, vaccinations, and managing infections. As a firm advocate for preventative care, I strive to empower parents with the information and support needed to make informed decisions about their children's health.

Throughout my career, I've witnessed the profound impact of early intervention and comprehensive care on a child's development. Join me on this journey as I continue to blend expertise with compassion, ensuring that every child receives the best start in life. Your child's health is my priority, and I look forward to contributing to their vibrant and healthy future.
